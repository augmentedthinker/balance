Balance Category

Home

Baseline Summary

The Home domain represents the environment where life unfolds. It includes the lived quality of your surroundings and whether they function as support, sanctuary, and a stable place from which to live.

Its subdimensions include living space organization, cleanliness and comfort, connection to nature, and a sense of sanctuary. Home therefore includes not only physical order, but also atmosphere and felt refuge.

Home matters because environment influences mental clarity, mood, and recovery. The spaces around you participate in shaping the life within you.
