Balance Category

Play

Baseline Summary

The Play domain represents creative expression and activities done for enjoyment. It is the part of life that allows for joy, experimentation, imagination, and activity that is not justified only by utility.

Its subdimensions include art and creative work, games and storytelling, and creative experimentation. These modes of play keep emotional and imaginative life from collapsing into pure obligation.

Play matters because it nourishes imagination and emotional vitality. It helps life stay alive rather than merely efficient.
