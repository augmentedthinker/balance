The Spirit domain represents introspection, philosophical reflection, and inner life. It is the dimension of life concerned with meaning, orientation, values, and the deeper interpretive layer through which the rest of life is understood.

Its subdimensions include journaling, meditation, philosophy, personal values, and purpose and meaning. These practices and concerns help a person process experience rather than merely accumulate it.

Spirit matters because reflection integrates experiences and guides intentional life direction. It is the central organizing domain that helps the rest of the system cohere.
